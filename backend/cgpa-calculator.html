Calculation Formula

1. Grade Point (GP): Calculated by dividing the Total Marks (Internal + External) obtained in a subject by 10.

GP = Total Marks / 10

2. Weighted Grade Points (WGP): Product of Grade Point and Subject Credits.

WGP = GP × Credits

3. SGPA / CGPA: Sum of Weighted Grade Points divided by the Total Credits.

SGPA/CGPA = Σ(WGP) / Σ(Credits)
